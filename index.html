How would a different universe sound?

The Überton Project aims to develop ready-to-use and open-source VST® audio plugins for instruments and sound effects based on concepts of theoretical physics.

In the virtual space we want to hear real sounds that are inaccessable by real instruments.

Sounds of the macro- and microcosmos

For example, n-dimensional vibrating bodies (such as bells or drums), sound propagation and echoing in n-dimensional rooms - or near a black hole - as well as vibrating strings (like a harp) so small that quantum effects enter as source of natural randomness.

The Überton project initially started with a academy course "Co(s)mic Sounds" and the first plugin was developed by J. Kipping, K. Levi, S. Lentner. The current development is supported by the Young Academy of Science and the University of Hamburg.
